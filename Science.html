Science

In Science we only offer courses to students in year 11 and 12. Here are the courses:

Physics

In physics for year 11 students learn and develop a foundation for the modules they become tested on inside of the HSC (year 12) we focus becoming skilled at these modules for year 11 and 12 to achieve band 6's inside of the HSC. Modules they cover include:

- Kinematics
- Dynamics
- Waves and thermodynamics
- Electricty and magnetism

These modules are essential to master not only for the HSC .
